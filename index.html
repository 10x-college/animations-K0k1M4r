Have Questions? We Have Answers.

Ease into the world of StyleLoom with clarity. Our FAQs cover a spectrum of topics.

How can I place an order on StyleLoom?

Ordering is easy! Simply browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to
checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your details and complete your purchase.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Unfortunately, once an order is confirmed, modifications or cancellations may not be
possible. Please review your order carefully before completing the purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking,
and select digital wallets. Choose the option that suits you best during checkout.

How do I initiate a return?

Visit our Returns page and follow the provided instructions. Ensure your item meets
our return criteria, and our team will guide you through the process.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use this
number to track your package in real-time on our website.

Do you offer exchanges for products?

At this time, we don't offer direct product exchanges. If you'd like a different item,
please initiate a return and place a new order.
